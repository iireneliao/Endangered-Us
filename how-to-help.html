How to help in your daily life!

  1. Learn about endangered species in your area
  2. Recycle plastic
  3. Visit a national wildlife refuge or park
  4. Stay on the official trial when hiking
  5. Do not use herbicides and pesticides
  6. Plant species that are native to your area
  7. Do not use palm oil
  8. Put bird feeders and other wildlife attractants, such as bird houses and baths
  9. Do not litter
  10. Reduce your use of water
