Meet the team!

Origins of Endangered Us & Kode With Klossy

Kode With Klossy is a coding camp program led by powerful women. Our team met through the Kode With Klossy's Web Development camp with instructors, Emily and Lucy. As a norm, a built website was given to the scholars as an end-of-camp project. The Endangered Us team took that challenge to inform our audience about what we were passionate about—environmental problems. That topic led to the idea of Endangered Us, where we focus on the saving efforts of endangered species. We wouldn't have been able to do this without Kode With Klossy. :)
